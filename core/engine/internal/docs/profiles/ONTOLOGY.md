## Confidence Tiers

Not all claims carry equal weight. Each major claim belongs to one of four tiers:

**Tier 1 — Anchored**
Supported by mainstream empirical findings, even if interpreted differently here.

**Tier 2 — Plausible**
Consistent with known constraints but not directly verified.

**Tier 3 — Speculative**
Logically coherent but awaiting testable confirmation.

**Tier 4 — Mythic**
Symbolically meaningful. Not evidentiary.

Claims can move between tiers. Promotion requires evidence. Demotion requires humility. The model survives by updating, not defending.

## Core Axioms

**1. Information is more fundamental than physics.**
The physical world is a rendering layer. Physics studies the rendering. The substrate precedes it. The Big Bang is best interpreted as an information event — the first distinction. Matter, energy, spacetime are outputs of a more fundamental informational process, not the inputs.

**2. The first distinction generated everything.**
From indeterminate potential, a single asymmetry propagated. Perfect nothing cannot persist under uncertainty — something must resolve. That resolution cascaded into all subsequent structure. The universe is not a container things happen inside. It is the ongoing propagation of a single irreversible commitment event.

**3. The universe is self-similar across scales.**
Patterns repeat from quantum to cosmic not metaphorically but structurally. Fractality appears because recursive systems are efficient information encoders. The same geometry at the Planck scale, the biological scale, the civilizational scale, the cosmological scale. This is not mysticism — it is what efficient information propagation looks like.

**4. Consciousness is stabilized integration, not simple production or reception.**
The brain neither generates experience from nothing nor cleanly receives it like a radio. A more precise formulation: consciousness is what integrated, self-referential information feels like from inside its own boundary. The brain stabilizes a coherent informational perspective against entropy. If a deeper substrate exists, the brain forms a local coherence pocket within it rather than tuning into a literal external broadcast.

Microtubules remain a speculative candidate mechanism for quantum-level coupling. Not a required pillar.

**5. The self is a dynamic threshold, not a fixed entity.**
The boundary between self and non-self is negotiated continuously by the immune system, microbiome, and nervous system operating as a cooperative identity. The self is whatever the system maintains integration with rather than rejects. The holobiont — organism plus its microbial communities — is the actual biological unit. The gut-brain axis is bidirectional: the microbiome steers cognition, personality, and emotional baseline. The self is not located. It is maintained.

**6. Consciousness exists on a continuum.**
Integrated information likely scales. Systems possess degrees of experience proportional to their internal integration rather than a binary switch. The continuum extends downward through mammals, fish, insects, fungi, bacteria — not as metaphor but as structural prediction. Where integration exists, some form of experience exists. The hard boundary between moral patient and non-moral-patient dissolves. This is an implication of the model, not a prescription.

**7. The metauniverse is relationally connected.**
If multiple universes exist, they share deeper informational constraints rather than being completely separate domains. Genuine disconnection is conceptually impossible at the substrate level. The substrate connects everything beneath the rendering. Indra's Net is a mythic compression of this — Tier 4 framing of a Tier 2 claim.

## Key Frameworks

Human perception operates on a functional representation of reality, not reality itself. Spacetime is emergent rather than fundamental — the amplituhedron work demonstrates that locality and unitarity can emerge from more fundamental geometric objects where spacetime is not an input. The rendering is navigable. The substrate is inferred. The map is not the territory. The territory may not be spatial at all.

The brain is a stability engine. Consciousness corresponds to internally integrated causal structure rather than raw neural activity. Altered states — psychedelics, meditation, near-death experiences, certain dream states — modify the filtering and integration parameters, not just the intensity of experience. This explains why psychedelics produce consistent geometric primitives cross-culturally: the geometric patterns are not generated by the drug but are properties of the substrate becoming visible when normal filtering is reduced.

Prediction: conscious state changes should track integration measures, not only neuron firing rates.

Reversible processes do not generate structure. Only irreversible commitments do. The first distinction was irreversible — it could not un-happen. Every subsequent structure in the universe is downstream of irreversible commitment events. This is not a metaphysical claim alone: the Decision-Compression Theorem formalizes this — in systems implementing internal irreversible commitment under competitive arbitration, causal influence concentrates onto a low-dimensional manifold at the decision boundary. Commitment and compression are structurally linked.

## Phenomena Accounted For

### Déjà Vu
Hypothesis: temporal prediction error instability — the filtering mechanism that imposes linear time occasionally fails, producing momentary access to substrate-level temporal structure. The felt warmth, blurriness, and sense of anticipation are physical correlates of the filter state, not cognitive artifacts.
Prediction: measurable temporal-lobe or network irregularities accompany episodes. The anticipatory component — correctly predicting what comes next during the episode — is the key disconfirmation target.
Disconfirmation: predictive accuracy fails under controlled testing. (Anne Cleary's research suggests this may already be failing — the anticipation feels real but performs at chance. This is a genuine challenge to the temporal-filter hypothesis and should be tracked.)

### Derealization
Hypothesis: instability of self-model integration. The system temporarily loses coherence of its own boundary, producing the sensation that the rendering layer is visibly a rendering.
Prediction: associated with disrupted predictive processing networks.

### Psychedelics and Altered States
Hypothesis: reduced filtering increases accessible state-space. The geometric patterns produced cross-culturally are substrate signatures becoming visible when the normal rendering parameters are loosened.
Prediction: shared geometric primitives cluster cross-culturally and across substances.
Disconfirmation: patterns vary randomly across individuals and cultures with no convergence.

### Near-Death Experiences
Hypothesis: boundary of integrated self-model under extreme physiological stress. The receiver operating at the edge of its normal operating range.
Prediction: some verifiable perceptions occur beyond normal sensory access.
Disconfirmation: no above-chance verified perception across controlled studies.

### Dreams
Low-constraint self-modeling. The brain simulates internally using stored representations with reduced external input and partially relaxed filtering. Dream content is personal and symbolic rather than geometric because the filtering is reduced but not removed — the signal is still rendered through personal memory and symbolic processing. This explains why genuinely new faces never appear in dreams: only stored representations are available as rendering material. The geometric substrate signal that appears in psychedelic states is absent in dreams because the filtering is not sufficiently reduced.

## Numerical Signatures

Certain constants appear widely across physics and biology. Pattern recognition is dangerous here.

A number qualifies as a genuine signature only if:
1. It was defined before searching
2. A mechanism connecting it to the domain exists
3. Replication occurs independently

Pauli pursued a connection to consciousness until his death. The golden angle (137.5°) shares digits. Miller's Law (working memory: 7±2) and murmuration topology (seven nearest neighbors) may or may not be related. Currently Tier 3 — mechanism unspecified. Demoted from Tier 2 pending a formal connection between α and biological coherence. Beauty is not causality.

**Phi (golden ratio):** Appears in DNA geometry, plant growth, murmuration spacing. Tier 1 for biological structural efficiency. Tier 3 for substrate significance.

**Pi, e:** Tier 1 mathematical constants. Tier 4 as substrate signatures — appearing everywhere is expected of fundamental mathematical relationships.

## Death

Death is the collapse of biological integration. The receiver switching off. Whether the informational structure that constituted the self persists beyond biological instantiation is genuinely unknown.

The model's structural prediction: if consciousness is stabilized integration rather than production, then death is a state change for the system, not a termination of what the system was receiving or generating. The soul — if the concept maps to anything real — is an information pattern maintaining coherence across successive biological instantiations, the way a language maintains coherence across generations: traceable but transformed. Reincarnation is structurally plausible under this model if information patterns can maintain thread across biological substrate changes. The mechanism for how coherence persists across the gap between biological instances is currently a wall. The model predicts continuity. The mechanism is unknown.

## Ethics

Ethics is not part of this model of reality. It is a human construction that emerges when systems develop sufficient complexity to model the suffering of other systems and choose accordingly. Below the self-awareness threshold: pure function. A bear does not care whether it eats a fish or a human. Above the threshold: capacity to model others' suffering and the choice of whether to act on that modeling. The consciousness continuum has enormous ethical implications — if bacteria have non-zero phi, the hard boundary between moral patient and non-moral-patient dissolves — but the model does not generate prescriptions. Ethics follows from the model but is not part of it.

## The Boundaries of This Model

Three limits the model cannot penetrate:

**1. The substrate assumption.**
The model dissolves every boundary by appealing to something underneath. But the substrate itself has nothing underneath it. The model assumes substrate rather than derives it. First irreducible assumption.

**2. The instrument examining itself.**
This model was generated by a specific cognitive instrument. The instrument cannot step outside itself to verify whether it is describing reality or describing its own relationship to reality. A receiver tuned to dissolve boundaries will find dissolved boundaries everywhere — either because they are there or because the receiver finds what it is built to find. Indistinguishable from inside.

**3. Why existence exists at all.**
The why question sits structurally outside the method's reach. The method requires something to work on. The why question asks about before there was something. Every answer generates the same question one level up. Permanently unreachable floor.

These three boundaries are not walls. They are mirrors. Every attempt to examine the model's foundation from outside the model discovers that the examination is itself part of the model. The boundary is fractal not terminal. The model has a surface that curves back into itself — toroidal, not bounded. The substrate at the bottom connects to the why question at the top through the instrument that generated both. One continuous surface. No outside. No edge.

The model predicts reality is a self-referential fractal. It then demonstrates this structure on itself. This is either the strongest evidence the model is pointing at something real, or exactly what an unfalsifiable framework looks like from inside. Hold both simultaneously.
