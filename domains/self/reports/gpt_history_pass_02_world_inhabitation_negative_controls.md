## Major Findings

The best Pass 2 compression is:

> Building matters when it changes a world that will remember it.

The operator is not simply attracted to complexity, crafting, lore, large collections, public articles, or shared universes. The stronger pattern is world-inhabitation under durability gates: a world becomes worth entering when it has depth, continuity, re-entry, safety, readable pressure, active memory, and a future that can receive the operator's changes.

A second major distinction emerged: clean leads are for public-facing and LLM-facing artifacts. Wikipedia, README files, prompts, and DISSONANCE.md need clean leads because they must orient other readers or models. Private worlds do not need this kind of lead. They need interiors that reward return.

## Clean Lead vs Private Depth

Public / LLM-facing structures need a clean lead:

- Wikipedia articles
- DISSONANCE.md
- README.md and domain READMEs
- agent prompts
- theory summaries
- public knowledge artifacts

Private / experiential worlds do not need clean leads:

- game worlds
- music worlds
- fictional universes
- private archives
- old internet rooms
- dream/surreal spaces
- Minecraft servers/worlds
- Trails
- Yume Nikki / PilotRedSun-style atmosphere

For private worlds, the key tests are depth, durability, continuity, re-entry, active memory, growth over time, safe invitation, and freedom from untrusted social volatility.

## Temporal Durability / Building Motivation

Candidate mechanism: **Temporal Contribution Gate**.

Building has low motivation when it is temporary construction, dead-server labor, self-expression alone, chores, or survival busywork. Building has high motivation when it reduces friction, provides safety, creates future usability, is appreciated across time, strengthens a lasting world, or supports other players/users/agents.

Minecraft is the clean central example: the operator is not motivated by Minecraft building in the abstract. Motivation rises when the server/world will stay up, remain active with players, and continuously grow. The build matters because later people and later states can remember it.

## Game Negative-Control Atlas

Counter-Strike violates the world-inhabitation gate: a simple bomb structure is not enough when the experience centers peeking, snipers, one-shot exposure, personal punishment, and low inhabitable depth.

Battle royale has low format pull. Fortnite is an exception only in a narrow way: the absurd cameo/cosmetic collision can be funny as surreal brand-crossover weirdness, not necessarily as gameplay attraction.

Fighting games and card games both trigger a duel-exposure problem. They concentrate opponent attention directly on the operator and convert play into personal/mechanical burden rather than an inhabitable field.

Rust, TTT, DarkRP, and many social survival sandboxes function as anxiety-factory controls: untrusted human volatility overwhelms invitation. Most survival/crafting games are weak because crafting/building alone is not enough; survival pressure often becomes chores or anxiety.

Terraria is the exception because exploration and depth can override survival/crafting aversion. The supplied 2015 capture supports this: the base is a durable, lit, organized, inhabited field with NPC rooms, display bands, storage/collections, and museum-like residue.

## Positive Contrast Anchors

- **Ace Combat**: structured military mission field, role/atmosphere, readable pressure.
- **SOCOM**: tactical military identity and mission field without random social-volatility anxiety.
- **Terraria**: exploration/depth plus bounded inhabited base-building.
- **Trails**: narrative-first continuity and world-memory.
- **Yume Nikki / PilotRedSun-style atmosphere**: private experiential spaces where re-entry and interior texture matter more than public-facing summary.

## Fiction / Universe Continuity

Hypothesis: Trails works as written world-memory; MCU/superhero continuity often fails as brand aggregation.

The contrast is not simply "big universe good/bad." Trails appears attractive because continuity is narrative-first: places, institutions, characters, and memory accumulate as a world. MCU/superhero movies are a negative-control candidate because the shared universe can feel like brands grouped together, with repetitive plot texture and grandness that is not earned by world-memory.

This should stay a hypothesis until more fiction examples are tested.

## Candidate DISSONANCE.md Additions

### Clean Lead vs Private Depth

Clean leads belong to public-facing or LLM-facing artifacts that need to orient a reader/model. Wikipedia needs a clean lead because it is public. DISSONANCE.md needs a clean lead because LLMs must understand the operator. Private/internal worlds do not need a clean lead; they need depth, continuity, re-entry, and inhabitable interiors.

### Temporal Contribution Gate

Building matters when it changes a world that will remember it. Temporary construction has low pull unless it improves future use, safety, continuity, or shared memory. Minecraft is motivating when the world/server will persist, remain active, and keep growing.

### Building as Friction Reduction / Safety

The operator does not build just to build. Building becomes motivating when it reduces friction, provides safety, creates future usability, is appreciated temporally, strengthens a lasting world, or supports other players/users/agents.

### Anxiety-Factory Games

Some games fail because they create untrusted exposure rather than inviting fields. Rust, TTT, DarkRP, many survival/social sandboxes, and Counter-Strike-like peeking/sniper exposure can become anxiety factories.

### Duel Exposure / Personal Mechanical Burden

Fighting games and card games can over-concentrate attention into a personal 1v1 duel. The issue is not simply difficulty; it is direct opponent focus plus mechanical/abstract burden without enough inhabitable field.

### Brand Aggregation vs Narrative Continuity

Shared universes do not automatically create continuity. Trails is a positive hypothesis because it is written as narrative/world-memory. MCU/superhero structure can read as brand aggregation and repeated plot texture rather than earned continuity.

### Encyclopedic Compression / Lead-Body Architecture

A lead is only good if the body can still reopen it. Public knowledge artifacts need clean compression that orients without destroying the body?s ability to restore detail.
