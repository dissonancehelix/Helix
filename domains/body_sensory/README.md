- Not medical diagnosis.

- Not raw health evidence storage.

## Core reward mechanics

- Localizable body maps.
- Controlled intensity.
- DOMS and stretch as readable signals.
- Massage and pressure when agency remains intact.
- Signal ownership.

## Core rejection mechanics

- Uncontrolled exposure.
- Intrusion without consent or handle.
- Vague body discomfort with no local map.
- External interpretation that overrides owned signal.
