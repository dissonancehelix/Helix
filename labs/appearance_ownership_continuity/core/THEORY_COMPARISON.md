# Theory Comparison

This comparison is a pressure tool, not a winner-take-all scoreboard. Owned Continuity Hypothesis (OCH) uses other theories by role: integration, access, perception, reflection, prediction, embodiment, and self-modeling may each explain part of an inhabited continuity without replacing the others.

IIT is treated here as a binding / intrinsic integration / interiority hypothesis, not as a total replacement for GNWT/GWT, Recurrent Processing Theory, Higher-Order Theory, Predictive Processing / Active Inference, embodied/enactive theories, or self-model theory.

## Working Division Of Labor

- IIT-like integration may help explain binding or intrinsic field formation.
- GNWT/GWT may explain access, report, and global availability.
- Recurrent Processing Theory may explain perceptual presence.
- Higher-Order Theory may explain reflective self-awareness.
- Predictive Processing / Active Inference may explain boundary and control coupling.
- Embodied / enactive theories may explain why body, action, and environment matter for owned selfhood.
- Self-model theory may explain subject-structure.
- OCH tests how these pieces combine into owned continuity.

## Guardrail

No row in this matrix is proof that a theory is correct. The matrix exists to prevent OCH from dissolving consciousness, selfhood, reportability, memory, intelligence, and behavior into one undifferentiated claim.
