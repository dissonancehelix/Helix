## 1. Purpose

Pain and bodily-need pressure made the valence/stakes lane harder to dismiss. The next question is whether interoception, homeostasis, and self-maintenance provide a scaffold for lived stakes without being identical to lived mattering.

This report tests the strongest conservative version:

> Interoceptive/homeostatic regulation may be a serious ownership-scaffold candidate because it binds bodily state, need, prediction, correction, urgency, and persistence. But regulation alone is not appearance, ownership, or lived mattering.

The origin-of-life analogy is used only as a lens: in life, boundary plus gradient plus self-maintenance plus persistence can create a new regime. In AOC, boundary plus interoception plus stakes plus temporal persistence may create a candidate ownership scaffold. But scaffold is not target. Life is not consciousness. Regulation is not lived mattering.

## 5. Why Interoception Is a Serious Scaffold Candidate

Interoception is stronger than generic input because it concerns the state of the bounded organism itself. It tracks variables that are not merely external facts but condition the system's ongoing viability: oxygen, hydration, hunger, temperature, inflammation, fatigue, nausea, heartbeat, visceral tension, and bodily threat.

This matters for AOC because owned valence often appears as more than neutral information. It has a `for-me` contour: needed by this body, bad for this body, relieving for this body, urgent from within this body.

Pressure gained:

- bodily need links state detection to organism-relative stakes
- urgency is not merely reportable content
- relief/satiety suggests valence texture beyond preference labels
- depersonalization/emotional numbing can weaken felt intimacy without erasing perception
- pain and need cases pressure accounts that reduce experience to external access or neutral self-modeling

But this remains scaffold pressure. Interoception can support lived stakes without being identical to lived stakes.

## 6. Why Homeostasis Is Not Lived Mattering

Homeostasis explains why a system regulates. It does not automatically explain why regulation feels like anything or why deviation is lived as bad-for-me.

A thermostat regulates. A cell regulates. A robot can monitor battery levels. A body can produce autonomic responses without report. A spinal/reflex pathway can withdraw. None of that is, by itself, evidence of lived mattering.

AOC separation:

- regulation = control relation over variables
- mattering = lived valence/concern from within
- ownership = mineness or bodily intimacy of the state
- appearance = presence from within at all

Homeostasis is close to the problem because it creates organism-relative stakes. It is not the solution because it can be described entirely in third-person control terms.

## 8. Rival-Theory Pressure

### Minimal Self

Minimal self gains pressure if interoceptive stakes show a thin pre-reflective `for-me` structure: hunger, breathlessness, pain, nausea, fatigue, and relief are not merely represented but imposed on a bodily subject.

Minimal self weakens if all such structure reduces cleanly to regulation, body modeling, report, or action tendency.

### Self-Model Theory

Self-Model Theory gains pressure if interoceptive/homeostatic states are best explained as represented body-state evaluations inside a model of the organism.

It weakens if lived urgency and bodily intimacy vary independently of explicit self-model content or if synthetic self-model labels fail the same pressure tests.

### Predictive Processing / Active Inference

Predictive processing gains pressure because interoception and allostasis are naturally expressed as prediction, precision, error correction, and action-oriented regulation.

It weakens if prediction/control explains adaptive regulation while leaving lived mattering and mineness as residual labels.

### Embodied / Enactive Accounts

Embodied/enactive views gain pressure because self-maintaining organism-world coupling is central to lived stakes.

They weaken if biological coupling is treated as sufficient for appearance or ownership without a bridge.

## 10. Level 2 Evaluation

Interoception/homeostasis produces meaningful Level 2 scaffold pressure because it improves discrimination among weak explanations:

- against report-only accounts: bodily stakes can exist before reflective report
- against responsiveness-only accounts: regulation and response do not equal lived mattering
- against reward/preference shortcuts: optimization is not pleasure
- against pure self-model labels: represented need is not automatically felt need
- against pure agency/control accounts: corrective behavior is not mineness
- against life/consciousness collapse: biological regulation is not sufficient for A0

It does not establish:

- A0
- a complete mechanism of lived valence
- artificial consciousness
- animal pain status by itself
- owner continuity
- minimal self as canon

Result: Level 2 scaffold pressure, not bridge closure.

## 13. Main Finding

Interoception/homeostasis is the best scaffold pressure route after pain/bodily need. It explains why lived stakes are not arbitrary decorative reports: bodily need is tied to bounded self-maintenance, regulation, prediction, and persistence.

But the central guardrail holds: regulation is not lived mattering. A system can regulate, correct, preserve, optimize, and respond without thereby establishing appearance, ownership, or felt concern.

This creates scaffold progress and one clean provisional guardrail candidate: `REGULATION_NOT_LIVED_MATTERING`.
