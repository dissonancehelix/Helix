# Reports

Reports in this folder are evidence review artifacts for the Owned Continuity harness.

They are not canon, not replacements for `../README.md`, and not replacements for `labs/appearance_ownership_continuity/THEORY.md`.

Use reports to:

- compare claims against empirical contact points,
- record thought-experiment runs,
- capture failed predictions,
- preserve negative controls,
- recommend claim promotion, demotion, or revision.

Reports become inputs to the claim ledger only after review.
