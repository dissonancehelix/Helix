## 2. Definitions

- **A0 bare appearance:** the remaining question of why there is anything it is like from within at all
- **appearance-from-within:** the difference between modeled, integrated, accessible, controllable, or reportable structure and lived presence
- **fielded appearance:** appearance organized as a structured field of content rather than disconnected fragments
- **ownership / mineness:** the mark by which an experience, action, body-state, memory, or perspective is mine
- **temporal flow:** lived succession across moments rather than mere sequence-tracking or clock-time coding
- **reportability:** availability of information for speech, action, reasoning, or communicable output
- **access:** operational availability for task control, flexible use, reasoning, or report
- **residue / marker:** an observable trace left by a process or state, useful for pressure but not identical to the target
- **continuity:** identity-bearing preservation through time
- **hard gap:** the remaining explanatory gap after neighboring proxies and correlates have been separated from the target
- **bridge candidate:** a proposed mechanism, structure, or process intended to connect neighboring functions to A0 rather than merely correlate with them

## 3. What Each Prior Layer Blocked

### A1 COGITATE

- **Proxy blocked:** content decoding, sustained activity, synchronization, task relevance, access/report, and integration-like markers as direct substitutes for appearance.
- **What remains useful:** A1 gives a disciplined field/access map and keeps content persistence distinct from reportability.
- **What remains unable to reach A0:** even strong content markers remain markers. They do not explain why content is present from within rather than only represented or decoded.

### A2 Ownership

- **Proxy blocked:** ownership collapsed into report, agency, body ownership, self-modeling, or reflective self-awareness.
- **What remains useful:** A2 shows that mineness is a real pressure lane and that altered ownership cases are informative rather than noise.
- **What remains unable to reach A0:** even if mineness is clarified, that still does not explain why anything appears at all.

### A3 Temporal Flow

- **Proxy blocked:** memory, narrative, smooth behavior, temporal coding, and reflective self-awareness as continuity carriers by default.
- **What remains useful:** A3 names lived succession as a stronger continuity candidate than public residue.
- **What remains unable to reach A0:** lived flow may be closer to continuity than memory is, but it still does not explain why there is appearance rather than only ordered processing.

### A4 Interruption / Re-entry

- **Proxy blocked:** delayed report, responsiveness, dose, recall, imagery-task success, and re-entry traces as direct evidence of the original field.
- **What remains useful:** A4 gives disciplined interruption/re-entry channels and improves falsifier terrain around report and response.
- **What remains unable to reach A0:** interruption traces remain residues or markers. They do not directly expose appearance during the interrupted interval.

### A5 Memory / Branching / Sleep / Upload

- **Proxy blocked:** memory, pattern, narrative, persona, behavioral continuity, copyability, and branch-insensitive similarity as same-owner proof.
- **What remains useful:** A5 sharply separates continuity support channels from singular first-person continuation.
- **What remains unable to reach A0:** even if same-owner continuity were solved, that would still not explain why there is appearance-from-within in the first place.

## 5. The Remaining A0 Shape

After proxy subtraction, A0 is still not:

- report
- access
- integration
- content decoding
- task relevance
- memory
- narrative
- self-report
- agency
- body ownership
- temporal coding
- behavioral continuity
- copyable pattern
- biological persistence

A0 still asks:

Why is there something it is like from within at all, rather than only modeled, integrated, accessible, reportable, controlled, remembered, or copied structure?

The hard-gap remainder is therefore narrower than before, but also less forgiving. Many of the nearby explanatory substitutes are now blocked. What remains is not "consciousness in general" as a blurred bundle, but the specific problem of appearance itself after field, ownership, flow, residue, and continuity have each been given their own lane.

## 8. What Would Count As Progress On A0?

### Level 0

Clearer proxy rejection.

### Level 1

A candidate bridge mechanism that explains why A0 is expected, not merely correlated.

### Level 2

A test that distinguishes candidate bridge mechanisms from access, report, ownership, temporal, or continuity alternatives.

### Level 3

A cross-domain marker or model that predicts presence, absence, or structure of appearance better than neighboring proxies.

### Level 4

A theory that explains why the marker entails appearance-from-within rather than merely tracking it.

AOC is currently mostly Level 0 to Level 1. It is strongest at refusing false bridges and weak-to-emergent at naming a positive one.

## 9. AOC Failure Modes

- treating proxy rejection as explanation
- treating no-bridge stance as victory
- overusing negative controls
- becoming too conceptual and not empirical enough
- turning AOC into taxonomy instead of test engine

## 10. Main Finding

AOC has not solved A0. Its current value is that it makes A0 sharper by stripping away nearby substitutes. The remaining hard gap is narrower, better-labeled, and better protected from false bridges, but still open.

The strongest positive result is epistemic discipline. The strongest negative result is that discipline alone does not explain appearance. AOC is closer to a clean question than to a completed answer.
