## 1. Purpose

The pain/bodily-need pressure report identified one narrow guardrail ready for provisional review: nociceptive signaling, reflex withdrawal, and physiological threat response must not be treated as identical to lived pain.

This patch preserves the useful pressure from pain without letting pain become a shortcut into A0. Pain can be a strong stakes surface without being a solved bridge. Nociception can be a biological support condition without being lived aversive appearance.

## 2. Proposed Guardrail

`NOCICEPTION_NOT_PAIN`

> Nociception, reflex withdrawal, and physiological threat response are not identical to lived pain.

This is a guardrail only. It does not explain pain. It does not explain A0. It does not decide animal pain or AI pain. It blocks a specific substitution error: signal/reflex/physiology = lived aversive ownership.

## 3. Shortcut Blocked

| Shortcut | Why it fails | AOC consequence |
| --- | --- | --- |
| nociception = pain | tissue-threat signaling can support pain without being the lived state itself | signal is not appearance |
| reflex withdrawal = suffering | withdrawal can occur as response/control without lived aversive ownership being established | responsiveness is not consciousness |
| physiological threat response = felt badness | arousal, stress markers, and regulation do not by themselves establish lived mattering | regulation is not lived mattering |
| pain report = pain | report remains access/output residue, not the target itself | report is not appearance |
| avoidance = owned aversion | action tendency can be dissociated from mineness and authorship | agency is not ownership |

## 4. Pressure Basis

Pain and bodily need remain important because they gather aversion, urgency, localization, relief, and bodily intimacy into one pressure family. They are stronger than decorative affect language.

But the pressure only helps if the target is kept clean:

- nociception is tissue-threat signaling
- reflex withdrawal is response/control
- physiological threat response is regulation/arousal
- pain report is report
- lived pain is aversive appearance owned from within

AOC should not let the first four impersonate the fifth.

## 6. Minimal Falsifier

The guardrail weakens if nociceptive signaling, reflex withdrawal, or physiological threat response fully predicts lived pain across report, no-report, dissociation, anesthesia, spinal/reflex, and synthetic-control cases with no residual distinction.

Until then, AOC should preserve the distinction.

## 8. Main Finding

`NOCICEPTION_NOT_PAIN` is narrow enough for provisional ledger review. It blocks a clean false-positive route created by the pain/bodily-need pressure surface: treating tissue-threat signaling, reflex response, or physiological regulation as lived aversive appearance.

This is guardrail progress, not mechanism progress. It makes hiding places smaller without pretending the bridge has been found.
