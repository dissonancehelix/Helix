## 1. Claim Under Pressure

Memory residue, report residue, pattern similarity, and behavioral continuity are not sufficient by themselves to establish same-self owned continuity.

Stronger claims not assumed:

- memory is not irrelevant
- pattern preservation is not irrelevant
- narrative continuity is not irrelevant
- biological continuity is not automatically sufficient
- AOC does not solve personal identity

## 2. Definitions From PRIMITIVES.md

- **continuity:** identity-bearing preservation through time
- **ownership:** the mark by which an experience, action, body-state, memory, or perspective is mine
- **identity preservation:** whether transformed continuity remains same-self rather than copy, fork, role, reconstruction, or replacement
- **memory:** remembered content, autobiographical trace, learned structure, or retrieval-supporting residue
- **residue:** what remains after a state, transition, or report surface; useful trace, not identical to the original state
- **branching:** a continuity threat where one prior stream yields multiple equally valid successor streams
- **transformation:** change absorbed by an ownership-stream while remaining recognizably the same self
- **reportability:** availability of information for speech, action, reasoning, or communicable output
- **appearance-from-within:** the difference between modeled/reported/integrated activity and there being something it is like from within
- **owned continuity:** selfhood as a continuity-bearing ownership-stream inside a conscious field

## 3. Prior Report Anchors

Prior reports already blocked several shortcuts:

- **A2 ownership absent:** ownership is nonunitary; report, agency, body ownership, and reflective selfhood do not collapse into one mineness channel.
- **A4 propofol:** responsiveness and task behavior are traces, not consciousness.
- **A4 DREAM:** report after waking is residue, not the original dream field.
- **A1 COGITATE:** content/access markers are not appearance itself.

A5 is the next pressure seam because once traces are separated from fielded appearance, continuity can no longer borrow authority from memory or report. If a dream report is not the dream field, and a content marker is not appearance, then autobiographical memory, copied narrative, or persistent behavior cannot automatically count as same-self survival.

The pressure result here is structural: every channel contributes something, but none of the public traces settles same-self owned continuity on its own.

## 6. Branching Pressure

Branching is the strongest A5 stressor because it breaks the easy shortcut from preserved information to preserved owner.

If one stream becomes two equally valid successors, then:

- memory duplication can preserve autobiographical residue in both successors,
- narrative continuity can remain available in both,
- behavior can remain similar in both,
- informational structure can remain high-fidelity in both,
- but singular first-person continuation becomes unclear.

This is why AOC/OCH keeps returning to non-branching owned continuity. The pressure claim is not that copies cannot be conscious. The pressure claim is narrower: duplicated memory and pattern do not automatically preserve one continuing owner in the ordinary singular sense.

What remains unresolved:

- whether non-branching dynamical continuity is required or only strongly favored
- whether gradual transformation preserves ownership better than discontinuous copying
- whether a destructive single-successor transfer differs in principle from a duplicated branch
- whether the question is empirically tractable or partly conceptual

## 9. A5 Pressure

This test can pressure:

- memory versus continuity
- residue versus survival
- branch/copy ambiguity
- transformation tolerance
- ownership-stream preservation
- report/narrative insufficiency

This test cannot pressure directly:

- A0 raw appearance
- neural mechanism of continuity
- metaphysical identity
- post-mortem survival
- whether uploading is possible
- whether a copy is conscious

The point is not to settle identity. The point is to stop memory, pattern, report, and narrative from wearing identity-preservation's badge by default.

## 10. Main Finding

A5 cannot be reduced to memory, report, pattern persistence, or narrative coherence. These are continuity residues or support channels, not evidence of same-self owned continuation. Branching remains the strongest unresolved pressure because duplicated memory can create multiple successor claims without preserving singular first-person continuity.

The most conservative A5 result is that continuity needs a stricter grammar than similarity. Similarity can survive copying. Owned continuity, if it exists, may need non-branching carriage through transformation rather than reconstructible resemblance after the fact.
