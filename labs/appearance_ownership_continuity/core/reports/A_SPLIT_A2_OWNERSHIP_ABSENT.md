## 1. Claim Under Pressure

Claim being tested:

> Ownership/mineness is separable from raw appearance, reportability, body-modeling, agency, and reflective self-awareness.

Stronger claim not assumed:

> Ownership is not being claimed as either necessary or unnecessary for A0 bare appearance.

This run tests whether A2 should remain a distinct layer. It does not decide whether a conscious field can exist with absolutely zero mineness.

## 2. Definitions From PRIMITIVES.md

- **Appearance-from-within:** the A gap between modeling/reporting/integrating/acting and there being something it is like from within.
- **Ownership / mineness:** the mark by which an experience, action, body-state, memory, or perspective is mine.
- **Self-model:** a model of the system as subject, perspective, body/control center, or continuity-bearing entity.
- **Embodiment:** coupling between experience, body or body-analogue, action, sensation, and world.
- **Agency/control coupling:** a link between perspective, possible action, prediction, and consequence.
- **Reportability:** availability of information for speech, action, reasoning, or communicable output.
- **Continuity:** identity-bearing preservation through time.

## 6. Main Finding

The conservative result is that A2 ownership is not one thing. Body ownership, agency, reflective self-awareness, report, and lived mineness can dissociate. These dissociations support A-Split and constrain any theory that collapses ownership into report, agency, self-modeling, or integration.

The strongest A2 pressure comes from the fact that altered ownership cases can preserve perception and report while changing mineness, body ownership, agency authorship, or reflective self-presence. The strongest caution is that these cases mostly pressure neighboring mechanisms; they do not directly inspect raw A0.

## 7. Gap Map

- Whether bare appearance can exist with zero mineness remains open.
- Whether minimal pre-reflective ownership is required for any conscious field remains open.
- Depersonalization may preserve a thin ownership layer even when ordinary mineness is severely altered.
- Body ownership and thought ownership may share mechanisms, partially overlap, or diverge.
- LLM self-report may be linguistic role simulation, functional self-model analogue, or future bridge candidate; current report alone is insufficient.
- The empirical marker that would distinguish owned appearance from mere report remains unresolved.

## 10. Verdict

- **What got stronger:** A2 as a separable layer; report-insufficiency; agency/ownership distinction; body ownership as local but not global identity evidence.
- **What got weaker:** any shortcut that equates "I" language, self-modeling, body schema, agency, or integration with owned mineness.
- **What remains open:** raw A0, zero-mineness consciousness, and the empirical marker for owned appearance.
- **What should be tested next:** `A_SPLIT_A2_AGENCY_WITHOUT_OWNERSHIP`, using alien limb / alien hand and agency illusion literature as the main pressure source.

Best compression:

> A2 is not the word "I"; it is the pressure point where appearance becomes mine, and several systems can imitate or disturb that pressure without settling A.
