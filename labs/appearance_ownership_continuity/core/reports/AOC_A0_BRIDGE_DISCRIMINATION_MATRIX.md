## 1. Purpose

The bridge scorecard ranked IIT and minimal-self/phenomenology as the
highest-value bridge candidates for different reasons. The IIT pressure report
kept IIT alive but constrained. This matrix asks what kinds of evidence or
contrast cases would actually discriminate bridge candidates from each other.

AOC is not choosing a winner. AOC is asking what would make a candidate earn
more bridge credit than its neighbors. The target is discriminating pressure,
not theory preference.

## 6. Pairwise Discrimination Questions

### IIT vs Minimal Self

Question:
Does formal intrinsic structure explain lived appearance, or does minimal
for-me-ness describe the target more directly but lack mechanism?

- What would favor IIT:
  - a test where intrinsic structure predicts conscious-field differences more
    specifically than phenomenological description alone
  - a case where IIT's structural distinctions survive report/access divergence
- What would favor Minimal Self:
  - a clean operational distinction between pre-reflective for-me-ness and
    self-model/report/body-ownership channels that IIT cannot explain
- What would leave both unresolved:
  - IIT stays structurally elegant but underexplanatory, while minimal-self
    language stays descriptively close but underoperationalized
- Best current or future test source:
  - `AOC_MINIMAL_SELF_OPERATIONALIZATION`

### IIT vs RPT

Question:
Is recurrent perceptual stabilization enough, or does integrated intrinsic
structure explain more than perceptual presence?

- What would favor IIT:
  - evidence that integrated structure explains beyond visual/perceptual
    recurrence and generalizes better across modalities or interruption cases
- What would favor RPT:
  - evidence that recurrent presence predicts conscious-perceptual structure
    while integration adds little beyond it
- What would leave both unresolved:
  - both explain A1-like field effects but neither reaches A0
- Best current or future test source:
  - `A_SPLIT_A1_COGITATE_MARKER_QUESTION_PLANNING`

### IIT vs GNWT

Question:
Does intrinsic field structure outperform access/report models where report and
field diverge?

- What would favor IIT:
  - no-report or low-report conditions where structured field markers remain
    better aligned with conscious-like presence than report/access availability
- What would favor GNWT:
  - cases where broadcast/access patterns explain all relevant variance and
    IIT-style field structure adds little
- What would leave both unresolved:
  - mixed marker support with no clear A0 discrimination
- Best current or future test source:
  - `A_SPLIT_A1_COGITATE_MARKER_QUESTION_PLANNING`

### IIT vs Predictive Processing

Question:
Does intrinsic integration explain appearance better than self-maintaining
prediction/control?

- What would favor IIT:
  - conditions where integrated intrinsic structure predicts presence better
    than control, regulation, or boundary maintenance
- What would favor Predictive Processing:
  - conditions where self-maintaining control architecture explains the relevant
    contrasts and integration does not add unique bridge value
- What would leave both unresolved:
  - each theory explains a neighboring lane but not A0
- Best current or future test source:
  - `AOC_PREDICTIVE_PROCESSING_BOUNDARY_PRESSURE`

### Minimal Self vs Self-Model

Question:
Can pre-reflective for-me-ness be separated from modeled self-representation?

- What would favor Minimal Self:
  - altered-ownership or flow cases where lived for-me-ness varies without
    corresponding change in explicit self-representation
- What would favor Self-Model Theory:
  - evidence that modeled self-structure cleanly predicts the relevant
    dissociations better than phenomenological categories
- What would leave both unresolved:
  - only report-level descriptions with no operational split
- Best current or future test source:
  - `AOC_MINIMAL_SELF_OPERATIONALIZATION`

### Temporal Flow vs Temporal Coding

Question:
Can lived duration be operationally distinguished from timing/sequence
processing?

- What would favor Temporal Flow:
  - a test where lived succession distinctions survive after timing, ordering,
    and memory-sequence explanations are blocked
- What would favor Temporal Coding:
  - all relevant temporal phenomena reduce cleanly to sequence, interval, or
    predictive timing markers
- What would leave both unresolved:
  - temporal language remains phenomenologically rich but operationally vague
- Best current or future test source:
  - `A_SPLIT_A3_FLOW_FALSE_POSITIVE_BATTERY`

### Valence vs Control

Question:
Does affective stake add A0-relevant lift beyond adaptive regulation?

- What would favor Valence:
  - a case where felt stakes explain something about presence not captured by
    control, access, or embodiment alone
- What would favor Control:
  - stake effects reduce to regulatory importance and adaptive behavior
- What would leave both unresolved:
  - valence helps explain mattering but not appearance
- Best current or future test source:
  - `AOC_VALENCE_STAKES_BRIDGE_PRESSURE`

### Embodiment vs Agency

Question:
Does sensorimotor coupling explain appearance or only control/ownership
structure?

- What would favor Embodiment:
  - evidence that situated bodily coupling explains more than agency or control
    channels do by themselves
- What would favor Agency/control accounts:
  - embodiment effects collapse into action prediction, boundary, or ownership
    structure
- What would leave both unresolved:
  - embodied perspective remains important but not bridge-specific
- Best current or future test source:
  - `AOC_PREDICTIVE_PROCESSING_BOUNDARY_PRESSURE`

## 9. What Would Count As Level 2 Bridge Progress?

To move from Level 1 to Level 2, a candidate would need:

- a discriminating test against at least two neighbor theories
- explicit blocking of known proxy collapses
- a predicted result specific enough to fail
- a result that would change claim maturity
- a pre-specified overclaiming route

Most plausible Level 2 path by candidate:

- IIT:
  - produce a test that discriminates integration/field predictions against
    both GNWT and RPT without leaning on report/access collapse
- Minimal Self:
  - produce an operational distinction against both Self-Model Theory and HOT
    that survives altered-ownership and flow cases
- RPT:
  - produce a test distinguishing recurrent perceptual presence from both GNWT
    broadcast and IIT integration in A1-like settings
- Predictive Processing:
  - produce a test distinguishing self-maintaining control from both valence and
    embodiment explanations while still claiming A0 relevance
- Valence:
  - produce a test distinguishing affective stakes from both regulatory control
    and report/access importance
- Temporal Flow:
  - produce a test distinguishing lived duration from both temporal coding and
    memory-sequence reconstruction

Current judgment: none has yet crossed into Level 2.

## 10. Main Finding

The leading bridge candidates remain Level 1 because none yet produces a clean
discriminating test that explains A0 rather than tracking a neighboring layer.
IIT has the strongest formal and empirical route. Minimal self has the closest
descriptive contact. RPT, predictive processing, valence, embodiment, and
temporal flow look more like strong bridge ingredients or neighbor theories
than complete A0 bridges.

The next progress step is a discriminating test design, not bridge promotion.
