### Metaphysical-why explanations

Metaphysical-why explanations ask why any mechanism is accompanied by appearance-from-within at all.

The A gap is the difference between:

```text
the system models, reports, integrates, predicts, and acts
```

and:

```text
there is something it is like from within
```

## 6. What Existing Theories Explain

### GNWT / GWT

Explains access, broadcast, flexible control, and reportability. It is strongest when information becomes globally available for speech, reasoning, or action.

Neighbor explained:

```text
what becomes available
```

Gap:

```text
why availability is appearance
```

### IIT

Potentially explains binding, intrinsic integration, and field unity. It remains the strongest comparison candidate for an interiority bridge, but only if integration can be shown to do more than redescribe structure.

Neighbor explained:

```text
why a system may be unified from its own causal structure
```

Gap:

```text
why intrinsic integration is appearance, and how ownership enters
```

### RPT

Explains recurrent perceptual presence and why some content may be phenomenally present without full report.

Neighbor explained:

```text
perceptual presence
```

Gap:

```text
appearance itself and self-continuity
```

### HOT

Explains reflective awareness and meta-representation.

Neighbor explained:

```text
awareness of being aware
```

Gap:

```text
pre-reflective appearance and ownership without explicit higher-order report
```

### Predictive Processing / Active Inference

Explains boundary, control coupling, uncertainty, body-world modeling, and action.

Neighbor explained:

```text
how a system maintains itself as bounded agent under uncertainty
```

Gap:

```text
why predictive control feels like anything
```

### Self-Model Theory

Explains subject structure, perspective, body ownership, and continuity modeling.

Neighbor explained:

```text
model of subject
```

Gap:

```text
inhabited subject rather than represented subject
```

## 7. What TOM / ICT Explains

TOM/ICT currently explains or sharpens:

- boundary/control as ownership evidence channels,


## 8. What Remains A

The strongest remaining A-gap:

```text
Why is there appearing rather than only integrated, accessible, controlled, self-modeled processing?
```

Even if a system has:

- global access,
- self-modeling,
- embodied control,
- temporal continuity,
- valenced stakes,
- memory,
- report,

the question remains:

```text

```

The strongest residue:

```text
Every neighbor explains a structure or function. A asks why any structure/function is lived.
```

## 10. Falsifiers / Pressure Tests

A-focused pressure would increase if:

- an empirical measure cleanly separated appearance from report, integration, and control across hard cases,
- a theory predicted no-report conscious presence better than alternatives,
- IIT-like intrinsic integration predicted field presence where access theories failed,
- RPT-like recurrent processing predicted perceptual presence without report,
- ownership alterations changed selfhood without changing basic appearance.

The current A framing weakens if:

- report/access fully predicts every credible consciousness case,
- integration alone explains appearance, ownership, and selfhood with no residue,
- predictive control explains not only function but why experience appears,
- self-modeling alone proves inhabited subjecthood,
- appearance-from-within becomes empirically reducible without field/boundary/ownership concepts.
