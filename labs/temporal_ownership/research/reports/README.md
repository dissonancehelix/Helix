# Research Reports

Reports here must explicitly state whether they pressure:

- TOM,
- ICT,
- DCP,
- LIP,
- EIP,
- or only provide analogy.

Each report should include:

- question,
- source or dataset,
- method,
- variables/proxies,
- result,
- claim pressure,
- what it strengthens,
- what it weakens,
- what it leaves undecided,
- false positives,
- false negatives,
- limitations,
- next test,
- canon-promotion recommendation, usually no.

Reports are pressure artifacts. They do not rewrite canon.
