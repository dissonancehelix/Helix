## Negative Controls

| Control | What it prevents |
|---|---|
| Likes complexity | Prevents treating size, difficulty, lore, or many folders as evidence of depth. |
| Completionism | Prevents treating finished games or large histories as meaningful without return/repair behavior. |
| Nostalgia | Prevents old screenshots, old web residue, and 2012 wiki history from counting just because they are old. |
| Hoarding | Prevents large collections from counting unless bounded by labels, storage, routes, or future use. |
| Convenience seeking | Prevents QoL mods from counting unless they remove waste friction while preserving meaningful agency. |
| Aesthetic preference | Prevents visual/cozy choices from counting unless they stabilize a field, improve inhabitation, or preserve grammar. |
| Social quantity | Prevents companions/NPCs/characters from counting unless they change field coherence or interiority. |

## Current Blocking Gates

- Global-pattern gate: require at least three independent evidence doors.
- Resolution gate: depth must improve orientation, prediction, repair, return,
  or agency.
- Abundance gate: plenty must be bounded by containers, categories, routes, or
  rituals.
- Vanilla-plus gate: additions must preserve or clarify the base grammar.
- Residue gate: authored trace must change interpretation or re-entry value.
- Friction gate: removed friction must be waste friction, not meaningful
  resistance.
