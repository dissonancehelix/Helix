# Inhabited Continuity Theory

**Provisional name.** Do not treat the name as settled.

**Status:** Conceptual framework and research harness. Not a proven theory. Not a finished argument. A structured set of primitives, claims, tests, and falsifiers designed to survive pressure rather than preserve a position.

---

## Main Question

> What makes a structured field become inhabited from within, and what must be preserved for that inhabited interior to remain the same self through time?

Two sub-questions that drive the work:

> Under what conditions does a structured process become not merely organized or intelligent, but experientially owned from the inside?

> How much can a system transform before the inhabited ownership-stream is no longer preserved?

## Core Thesis (one sentence)

> A self is an owned continuity-stream inside a conscious field, preserved through transformation by boundary, integration, self-modeling, and non-branching temporal structure.

Compressed further:

> Consciousness is appearance. Self is owned continuity.
