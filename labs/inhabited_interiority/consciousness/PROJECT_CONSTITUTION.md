# Project Constitution
## Inhabited Interiority Research Program

Version: 1.0  
Status: Active  
Last revised: 2026-04-26

---

### What this project is

A single-person, long-running research program studying whether consciousness can be
modeled as inhabited interiority, and whether self-continuity can be modeled as
inhabited ownership preserved through non-branching temporal transformation.

It is a falsification and classification harness, not a proof of the theory.

---

### Governing principles

**1. This project does not claim to solve consciousness.**
It constructs a falsifiable model, stress-tests it against edge cases and rival theories,
and tracks which claims survive, weaken, break, or remain unresolved.

**2. This project studies whether consciousness can be modeled as inhabited interiority.**
Not whether consciousness *is* inhabited interiority. The model may be wrong in multiple
ways that only become visible through systematic testing.

**3. This project studies whether self-continuity can be modeled as inhabited ownership
preserved through non-branching temporal transformation.**
Pattern survival, memory continuity, and symbolic continuity are not automatically
self-survival. The distinction must be tested, not assumed.

**4. Givenness / appearance-from-within may be primitive.**
G may be the point where conceptual analysis stops and empirical proxy testing begins.
The project does not pretend to detect G directly. It classifies candidate evidence,
proxies, edge cases, and false positives.

**5. Field-like structure is not automatically phenomenal.**
Markets, institutions, games, sports, workspaces, and fictional worlds are false-positive
controls. Intelligence, information integration, behavioral coherence, and symbolic
self-reference do not establish G.

**6. False positives are central to the research program, not optional.**
Every positive classification claim must survive a false-positive scan. The false-positive
atlas is as important as the case battery.

**7. Pattern survival is not field survival.**
A pattern can be preserved through transformation while the field that carried it is gone.
The two must not be conflated.

**8. Field survival is not automatically self survival.**
A phenomenal field may persist while self-continuity is lost (branching, ownership collapse).
C > 0 does not entail S > 0.

**9. Branching is fatal to singular self-continuity.**
When N = 0 (branching occurs), S = 0 by formula and by definition. No amount of preserved
pattern, memory, or constraint restores singular self-continuity after branching. This is
not an assumption to be revisited — it is a definitional hard stop.

**10. A result that weakens or breaks a claim is progress.**
The project becomes stronger by losing false claims. Preserved breaks are research output.
Patching a break with prose is not progress.

**11. No claim may be promoted without surviving edge cases, rival theory comparison,
and false-positive tests.**
Promotion requires demonstrated survival across the full test battery, not just
internal coherence of the model.

**12. The project must never absorb every failure as confirmation.**
The failure mode called "unfalsifiable_absorption" — where every disconfirmation is
reinterpreted as evidence for the theory — is a kill condition. If the project reaches
this state, it must mark the central claims as broken and restart from a more restricted
position.

---

### What this project must not do

- Claim to prove consciousness exists in any system
- Claim to prove consciousness is absent in any system
- Infer G from intelligence, language, self-report, memory, field structure, symbolic continuity, agency, optimization, social coordination, or complexity
- Patch a preserved break with prose or by inflating scores
- Mark any claim as proven or confirmed
- Expand the theory to absorb more phenomena without tightening its falsifiers
- Build elegant architecture without filling it with tested cases

### Research style sources

This program draws methodology from:

- **Darwin**: Home laboratory. Many small observations over time. Willingness to sit
  with difficulty without forcing resolution. Build incrementally; publish when the case
  is genuinely strong.

- **Gwern**: Long-term public notebook. Confidence tags. Checklists. Rerunnable analysis.
  The work is the record, not a cleaned-up presentation of the work.

- **Grothendieck**: Research-program document. The rising sea approach — surround the
  problem until it has no choice but to yield. But applied here with falsificationist
  discipline.

- **Ramanujan**: Raw conjecture capture before proof. Record what seems true and mark
  the proof debt explicitly. Do not mistake conjecture coherence for demonstrated truth.

- **Wolfram**: Systematic exploration of possible systems as empirical objects. The
  space of possible theories is itself an object of study.

- **Seth Roberts**: Self-experimentation as hypothesis generation with controls. But
  with stronger controls here — single-person research does not excuse weaker standards.

These are style sources, not endorsements of their epistemic commitments wholesale.
