# Self-Confirmation Blocker (Truth Layer)

## 1. Scope
This rule blocks self-confirmation and agreement-bias from being used as structural evidence.

## 2. Fundamental Rule
If:
- Two mathematical models, projections, or algorithms **AGREE**.
AND
- **Adversarial (Structural Hostility) Tests** have not been run.
THEN:
- Confidence score is strictly **ZERO**.
- The agreement is deemed **COINCIDENTAL** or an **ENCODING ARTIFACT**.

## 3. Evidence of Truth
Agreement between models is not evidence. Only **INVARIANCE UNDER HOSTILITY** increases the probability of structural truth.

## 4. Application
This rule applies to all automated manifold discovery and transfer feasibility calculations. No transfer or manifold claim is promoted based on "alignment" alone.
