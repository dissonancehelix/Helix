# Resolution > Minimality Policy

## 1. Primary Mandate
Discovery resolution is the primary objective of the Helix instrument. Elegance, minimality, and symmetry are secondary and MUST NEVER suppress measurable resolution gain.

## 2. Definition of Resolution Gain
Resolution Gain is calculated as:
  **+ Decrease in False Twin Collisions**: Reduction in distinct domains mapped to identical or near-identical coordinates.
  **+ Decrease in Persistent Isolates**: Successful integration of previously isolated domains into meaningful clusters or transfers.
  **+ Increase in Feasible Transfer Precision**: Improved accuracy in identifying mechanism-compatible transfer neighbors.
  **+ Improvement in Predictive Accuracy**: Higher success in predicting pathological regimes or boundary collapses.

## 3. Expansion Permit
If **Resolution Gain > 0** and the candidate has survived **structural hostility** tests:
- Expansion of the current ontology is **PERMITTED** and **ENCOURAGED**.
- Minimality claims MUST yield to resolution evidence.

## 4. Elimination of Aesthetic Inertia
Calculations of "minimal basis sets" for purely aesthetic or symbolic reasons are inadmissible as grounds for rejecting higher-resolution ontological candidates.
