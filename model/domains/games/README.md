# Domain: Games

## Domain Role

Games are a first-class externalized cognition domain because the operator has played, archived, modded, mapped, completed, analyzed, and acted through game systems. This domain predicts fit from field mechanics, not genre.

## External / Operational Model

The relevant unit is the playable field: state transitions, pressure, roles, topology, denial, support, danger, return value, and guide-layer understanding.

Central surfaces:

- **Extreme Football Throwdown** as the clearest competitive/externalized game cognition surface
- **GMod** playtime anchored to EFT, mapmaking, and modded-community re-entry, not generic sandbox preference
- Steam/playtime/completion evidence as behavioral archive
- VMF-map analysis and future map/field ingestion
- guide-layer game understanding: walkthroughs, route logic, mechanics, roles, and operational interiors

## Dissonance Mapping

Upward links:

- `field_mechanics_over_genre`

Fit predictors:

- state-transition mastery
- field-scale leverage
- authored danger with readable rules
- support/denial roles that shape the whole fight
- guide-layer payoff: the game becomes richer when its mechanics are understood
- completion/playtime split: finishing is evidence, but long playtime often marks reusable system-depth

Role anchors:

- **Moira**: room geometry, sustain pressure, damage/healing conversion, Fade re-entry
- **Ana**: backline rule editing through sleep, anti-heal, aim, and timing
- **Zenyatta**: vulnerability marking and fragile backline pressure
- **Dark Seer**: topology, Ion Shell pressure, Vacuum/Wall fight-shaping
- **Bane**: removing one actor from the field
- **Axe**: converting enemy agency into forced commitment
- **Ed Reed**: backline denial posture and future-state theft
- **Jayden Daniels / Commanders**: hope as live possibility-space and long-horizon investment

## False Positives

- support means healing
- difficulty means difficulty-as-sport
- open world means checklist sprawl
- streamer means parasocial center; creator is a doorway, not the billboard
- GMod means generic sandbox preference
- social deception means mystery fit; hidden social rules and performative lying can be hostile
