# Wiki Domain

## Domain Role

Wiki is a first-class externalized cognition domain because Wikipedia editing turns knowledge into public architecture: article structure, source hierarchy, citation logic, templates, infoboxes, and future-reader inference.

## External / Operational Model

Wikipedia work is not just writing. It is public knowledge compression under rules:

- article architecture and lead structure
- citation/source structure
- infobox/template logic
- neutral prose with structural clarity
- formal compression that lets future readers infer the subject's shape with low friction
- maintenance burden control: cleverness is bad if it makes the article harder to preserve

Language/Wikipedia overlap belongs here when it concerns article prose, public knowledge structure, citation grammar, template logic, or reader inference. Spanish learning and personal language constraints belong in `self/` unless a language app is reopened.
