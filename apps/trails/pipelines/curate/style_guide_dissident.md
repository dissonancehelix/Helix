# Helix Trails Domain: Tone Manifest (Encyclopedic Guidelines)

This manifest provides stylistic **guidelines** inspired by the Wikipedia and cognitive writing style of **Dissident93**. It is meant to ensure high structural legibility and neutrality, but it should **not** act as a suffocating, unbreakable policy. The goal is clarity, not rigid limitation.

## 1. Structural Guidelines (The Sentinel Concept)

### Lead Construction
*   **The Identity statement**: The first sentence should ideally act as a high-compression identity statement.
    *   *Standard Example*: `'''Name''' is a [Role] in [Series/Context].`
*   **Architecture over Content**: Favor functional descriptors over subjective adjectives (e.g., use "operating as a pivot" instead of "mysterious").

### State Transition & Trajectory
*   **Arc-based Transitions**: Characters are generally well-defined by tracking their transitions and growth across arc boundaries.
    *   *Example*: "Her professional trajectory follows her transition from a rookie in the Liberl arc to a recurring operational ally in the Erebonian crisis."
*   **Knowledge as Progression**: Focus on what the character *knows* or *reveals* about the world's hidden architecture.

## 2. Prose Guidelines (The Disciplined Voice)

*   **Disciplined Prose**: Use "precision without unnecessary ornament." Strip away noise like "It is noted that" or "Many players feel."
*   **Indifferent Observer**: The tone must remain indifferent to the subject's ego. The character exists within a world that "does not flatter the player."
*   **Factual Compression**: Reduction of large factual surfaces into tighter prose without losing the subject's **load-bearing structure**.
*   **Wikitext Hygiene**: 
    *   Bold the primary name on first mention.
    *   Italicize games and media titles.
    *   NO redundant templates or brackets.

## 3. The "Anti-Cringe" Filter (Neutrality Baseline)
The substrate must remain **strictly third-person**. 
*   **NO**: "You first meet him in..."
*   **YES**: "He is introduced in..."
*   **NO**: "Fan-favorite," "Beloved," "Strongest."
*   **YES**: "Recurring," "Key," "High-leverage."

## 4. Verification Specimen (Encyclopedic Standard)

> '''Van Arkride''' is the protagonist of the *Daybreak* arc. He operates as a Spriggan based in Calvard, specializing in work that falls between the jurisdiction of the police and the Bracer Guild. His professional trajectory centers on the resolution of case files involving factions such as Almata, maintaining a position of leveraged neutrality within the Republic's underground.
