## Boundary Prediction Capacity
EIP specifies when measurement collapse occurs due to unrecoverable correlations with the environment, but it does NOT geometrically predict whether that threshold will manifest physically as a combinatorial syndrome failure, a continuous bifurcation, or a singular divergence. Its prediction on boundary type provides no information gain beyond what the substrate geometry already dictated.
